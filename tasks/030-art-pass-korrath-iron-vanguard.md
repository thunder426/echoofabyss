## Description

Generate and wire art for the 5 cards + 1 token in `korrath_iron_vanguard` (designed in §12). Iron Vanguard's visual identity is heavy plate armour, disciplined human formations, banners-and-pikes Roman-style legion aesthetic.

Minions (card + battlefield):
- `shield_squire` — young Human with oversized shield.
- `vanguard_marshal` — Human commander, scroll/banner motif for the card-draw flavor.
- `lord_commander` — Human, regal full-plate, projection-of-authority pose. Should feel like a hero-armour-granting figure.

Spells (card only):
- `shield_bash` — concussive shield-strike motif, scales-with-Armour visual cue.
- `oath_of_iron` — ritual oath being sworn, Footmen materializing in the background.

Token (card-face only):
- `iron_footman` — distinct from Order Footman (core pool's token). Iron Footman is 200/200 vs Order Footman's 100/200 — visually depict heavier armour to signal the stat difference.
