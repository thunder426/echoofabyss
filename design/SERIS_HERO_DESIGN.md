## Seris, the Fleshbinder — Passives & Talents

---

### Hero Identity
**Name:** Seris, the Fleshbinder
**Faction:** Abyss Order
**Resource System:** Standard Essence / Mana

---

### New Mechanic — Flesh
A passive counter that charges whenever a friendly Demon dies (by any means — combat, sacrifice, enemy removal). Maximum 5. Resets to 0 at the start of each combat. Spent by talent abilities and support cards.

---

### Permanent Passives

| Passive | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Fleshbind** | Whenever a friendly Demon dies, gain 1 Flesh (max 5). Resets each combat |
| **Grafted Affinity** | May include up to 4 copies of Grafted Fiend in the deck (default cap is 2) |

---

### New Keyword — Siphon
This minion restores HP to itself equal to 50% of the damage it deals. Distinct from Lifedrain which heals the hero.

---

*Empower Fiends on entry, grow through kills, protect as long-term investment*

| Tier | ID | Name | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| T0 | flesh_infusion | Flesh Infusion | When you play a Grafted Fiend, spend 1 Flesh to give it +200 ATK permanently |

| T2 | predatory_surge | Predatory Surge | Grafted Fiends enter with Swift. When a Grafted Fiend reaches 3 kill stacks, it permanently gains Siphon |
| T3 | deathless_flesh | Deathless Flesh | CAPSTONE: When a Grafted Fiend would die, spend 2 Flesh instead. If fewer than 2 Flesh, it dies normally |

### Branch 3 — Corruption Engine
*Stack Corruption on Fiends as an ATK buff, detonate for spell synergy and Flesh generation*

| Tier | ID | Name | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| T0 | corrupt_flesh | Corrupt Flesh | Corruption stacks on friendly Demons grant +100 ATK per stack instead of −100. Spend 1 Flesh: apply 1 Corruption stack to a chosen friendly Demon. If target is a Grafted Fiend, apply 2 stacks instead |
| T1 | corrupt_detonation | Corrupt Detonation | Whenever Corruption stacks are removed from a friendly Demon by any means (death, Purge, Cleanse, or enemy effect), deal 100 damage per stack to a random enemy |

---

### Key Rules
- Fleshbind fires on **any** Demon death regardless of cause
- Flesh-summoned Fiends via Soul Forge are full Grafted Fiend instances — they grow via Grafted Constitution if Fleshcraft is active
- Lesser Demon and Forged Demon both trigger Fleshbind on death
- Lesser Demon is not a Grafted Fiend — Grafted Constitution never applies to it
- Siphon heals the **minion itself**, not the hero
- Corruption Engine T0 inverts Corruption on friendly Demons only — enemy Corruption still reduces ATK normally
